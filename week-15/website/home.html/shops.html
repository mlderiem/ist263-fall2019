Where to Shop in New York City

Restoration Hardware (Chelsea)

You may have visited a Restoration Hardware previously, but this one is unlike anything you've experienced. The glass elevator will bring you up to the fifth floor where you can dine or enjoy the rooftop- this is furniture shopping like you've never experienced!

November 19th

This privately owned boutique is tucked right in the middle of Hell's Kitchen. The somewhat crowded yet charming store is stocked with beautiful wallets, stunning scents, and thoughtful cards, among many other treasures. If I am ever in the area, I alwways make an effort to come.

Kith Flagship

Kith is located in Soho and gives the shopper a whole new experience. From the high ceilings to architecturally intruiging displays, you will be sure to love the Kith experience. While you're there, visit the ice cream counter!
