Where to Eat in New York City


For a fancier night out...

Public House Restaurant

The Public House Hotel is the perfect spot for dinner if you want a fancy yet fun setting before a night out in the East Village. After spotting the iconic red escalator on your way in, head downstairs to the restaurant to be greeted with high ceilings and a cool ambiance. If you go, make sure to order the fried seafood appetizer and taste the fried lemon!


For an unforgettable dining experience...

Dirt Candy

This was one of my favorite meals I've ever had- partially because the food was incredible, but also because the dining experience was completely unique. At Dirt Candy, your vegetarian-based meal comes in six courses. Expect to be wow-ed by DIY lettuce tacos and melting mushroom chocolate for dessert. If you want extra brownie points, read the illustrated "Dirt Candy: The Cookbook" before visiting!
